Luke Skywalker

From Farmboy to Jedi Grandmaster

Timeline of Luke Skywalker's life

- Hero of the Rebellion -

  • 19BBY- Luke Skywalker was born on the asteroid colony Polis Massa
  • 0ABYLuke Skywalker leaves Tatooine with Ben Kenobi after Imperial Stormtroopers killed his aunt and uncle. Ben Kenobi hired the smuggler Han Solo and his companion Chewbacca and the group escaped Imperial Capture
  • 0ABY- Luke and his group were captured aboard the Death Star upon arriving on the Alderaan system. The group managed rescue Princess Leia, who was being held prisoner aboard. They managed to escape the Death Star but at the cost of Ben Kenobi sacrificing his life.
  • 0ABY- Luke Skywalker joins the Rebel Alliance on the planet of Yavin and takes part in the Battle of Yavin, where he with the assistance of Han and Chewbacca, fired the shot that destroyed the Death Star, thus becoming Hero of the Rebllion
  • 0ABY-3ABY- Luke continued to fly many missions for the Rebel Alliance following the destruction of the Death Star and eventually led the elite fighter pilot squadron Rogue squadron
    • , who would later go on to be one of the most deadly fighter squadrons in the Galaxy.
  • 3ABY- Luke would get a vision from his former mentor Bend Kenobi, telling him to seek out Jedi Master Yoda in the Dagobah system to further his Jedi training.
  • 3ABY- Luke went to seek out Yoda, who at first was unwilling to train Luke but eventually would agree. But before Luke could finish his training, he had a vision in which he saw his friends in trouble and rashly left despite disapproval from both Yoda and Ben Kenobi
  • 3ABY- Luke arrived on Cloud City and fought Darth Vader, whom he was defeated by after Vader cut of his right hand. Vader revealed that he was Luke's father and offered Luke to join him, in which he refused and was saved by Princess Leia who heard his plea for help through the force. After they reunited with the rebel fleet, Luke received a cybernetic hand to replace the hand he lost.
  • 4ABY- Luke Skywalker returned to Tatooine to rescue Han Solo and his other friends from Jabba the Hut. The group was successful in the rescue, destroyed both Jabba and his barge then escaping through a sandstorm.
  • 4ABY- Shortly after the rescue Luke returned to Dagobah to complete his training with Yoda. Luke confirmed with both Yoda and Kenobi, that his father was indeed Darth Vader. Yoda dying, told Luke that his training was complete but in order to become a Jedi, he would need to face Vader once more. Luke also realized that Princess Leia was his sister and would be in great danger if Vader discovered the truth.
  • 4ABY- Luke would accompany his friends on the mission to Endor, where they needed to knock out the shield generator that protected the second Death Star in orbit. Upon arrival in the system Luke sensed Vader's presence on a nearby Star Destroyed and realized his presence dangered his friends and the mission. On Endor, Luke revealed to Leia Organa her true family heritage and his choice to face Vader. Shortly afterwards, Luke surrendered to Vader in an attempt to bring his father back to the light, but ended up becoming a prisoner of the Emperor

-- Jedi Master Mara Jade Skywalker
